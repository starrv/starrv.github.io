About Me

I am currently self employed publishing my own apps and about to start a YouTube channel to educate others about technology. I obtained my B.S. of Computer Science from CUNY Lehman College. I worked as a PHP Developer Intern and co-founded and became the Vice President of the NSBE chapter at Lehman while I was a student.

I taught Python and web development courses to youth, young adults, and seniors who were interested in the possibility of entering the tech industry. A young man who was interested in starting a startup found my profile on LinkedIn and asked me to help him build and publish a mobile application for him. So far I added authentication features and contributed to database design. Outside of work I attend tech meetups to network and learn about the latest technology.
