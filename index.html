About Me

I am a Software Engineer with experience building and publishing desktop, mobile, and web applications. I use various technologies including Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, PHP, SQL, Python, Amazon Web Services, and Google Cloud Platform.

I have a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from CUNY Lehman College and a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics from Union College. As an undergraduate at Lehman College I worked as a Full-Stack Web Developer Intern. I also co-founded and became Vice President of the NSBE chapter at Lehman College. After graduation I completed an internship where I learned about the MeanStack framework and worked at Revature as a Java Developer intern.

Over the years I taught numerous courses in Python and web development to middle schoolers, high schoolers, young adults, and seniors. I built and published applications of my own. I also worked with startup organizations, helping them build and publish their applications.

Outside of work I attend tech meetups, conferences, and hackathons. For my next role I am looking to work as a Software Engineer making significant contributions to innovative projects. Please click this link to view my resume.
