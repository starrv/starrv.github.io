About Me

I am a Software Engineer with experience building and publishing desktop, mobile, and web applications. I use various technologies including Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, PHP, SQL, Python, Amazon Web Services, and Google Cloud Platform.

I have a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from CUNY Lehman College and a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics from Union College. As an undergraduate at Lehman College I worked as a Full-Stack Web Developer Intern. I also co-founded and became Vice President of the NSBE chapter at Lehman College. I graduated from Lehman College with a 3.8 GPA with Departmental Honors in Mathetics and Computer Science. Immediately after graduation I completed an internship where I learned about the MeanStack framework and worked at Revature as a Java Developer intern where I learned about Spring and Hibernate.

Over the years I taught numerous courses in Python and application development to middle schoolers, high schoolers, young adults, and seniors. I also worked with startup organizations, helping them build and publish their applications. In addition, I built and published applications of my own on various platforms.

Outside of work I attend tech meetups, conferences, and hackathons. You can view my resume via this link.
