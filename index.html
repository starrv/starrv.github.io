About Me

I am a Software Engineer who has experience building applications in various programming languages and taught coding courses to youth, young adults, and seniors. I recently published my own applications on the Windows Store and Google Play Store and plan on publishing apps on the MacOS/iOS platform in the near future.

I am working on starting a YouTube channel where I make videos teaching others how to code and build applications. I obtained my B.S. of Computer Science from CUNY Lehman College. I worked as a PHP Developer Intern and co-founded and became the Vice President of the NSBE chapter at Lehman while I was a student.

After graduation I attended a training program where I learned about the Meanstack development platform and completed a summer internship at Revature as a Java Developer Intern. I teach Python and web development courses to youth, young adults, and seniors who are interested in the possibility of entering the tech industry. Outside of work I attend tech meetups to network and learn about the latest technology. I also occasionally work with startups and small teams on a project.
