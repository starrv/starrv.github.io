About Me

I am a Software Engineer who has a lot of experience building and publishing desktop, mobile, and web applications using various technologies including Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, PHP, SQL, Heroku, AWS, and Flutter.

I have a B.S. in Computer Science from CUNY Lehman College. As an undergraduate I worked an internship as a PHP Developer and co-founded and became Vice President of the NSBE chapter at Lehman College. After graduation I completed an internship where I learned about the MeanStack framework, worked at Revature as a Java Developer intern, taught numerous courses in Python and web development to middle schoolers, high schoolers, young adults, and seniors. I have also built and published applications of my own. I worked with start ups and helped them build and publish their applications.

Outside of work I attend tech meetups, conferences, and hackathons. I would love to get a chance to work on an interesting project. Please click this link to view my resume.
